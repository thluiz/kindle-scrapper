> Once upon a time, a Budo Master observed a sad cat gazing at the mountain. Felling sorry for the cat, the Master asked him: “What’s going on, noble feline?” The cat answered: “I’m searching for happiness!” The Master: “If that’s what you are looking for, it is right there... It is your tale!” The cat immediately jumped and spun, and spun, and spun, trying to catch its own tale. After a while, exhausted, the cat laid down and said: “Master, it is impossible to grab happiness!”. The Master: “Your tale is your happiness... and where is it?” “Right behind me”, replied the cat. The Master invited the cat to join him on part of the road, towards the Dojo. Together they took upon the walk. After about half mile in, the Master asked: “Where is happiness?”... The cat looked back, saw its tale and answered “Look, Master... it is walking along with us!”
